Special Characters and HTML Comments

Special Characters

Here are some examples of special characters using HTML entities:

  • & : This is the ampersand (&) character.
  • < : This is the less-than (<) character.
  • > : This is the greater-than (>) character.
  • © : This is the copyright (©) symbol.
  • ® : This is the registered trademark (®) symbol.
  • ™ : This is the trademark (™) symbol.
